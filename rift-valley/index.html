🌍 Rift Valley Lakes of Kenya

The Great Rift Valley in Kenya is home to a series of lakes, each with unique beauty and ecological importance.

...
...

🌊 Lake Turkana

The world’s largest desert lake, known for its greenish-blue color and prehistoric significance.

🌊 Lake Baringo

A freshwater lake with rich birdlife and home to crocodiles and hippos.

🌊 Lake Bogoria

Famous for its hot springs and large flocks of flamingos.

🌊 Lake Nakuru

Known for flamingos, rhinos, and stunning views inside a national park.

🌊 Lake Naivasha

A freshwater lake popular for boat rides and surrounded by wildlife.

🌊 Lake Elementaita

A shallow soda lake attracting flamingos and pelicans.

🌊 Lake Magadi

One of the saltiest lakes in Kenya, surrounded by dramatic landscapes and hot springs.
